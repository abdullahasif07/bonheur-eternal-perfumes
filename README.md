# BONHEURETERNAL

**Fragrance, eternalized.**

BONHEURETERNAL is a luxury fragrance house imagined as a quiet antidote to the disposable: scents composed slowly, from materials chosen with care, and offered as small archives of mood, memory, and place. This project is its **digital home**—a single, calm page that introduces the line, invites discovery, and opens a direct conversation with the people behind the bottles.

---

## What this website is for

The site is **branding and lead generation only**. There is no shopping cart, no checkout, and no payment flow. Visitors learn who BONHEURETERNAL is, explore a curated **signature collection**, read a concise **story**, and reach out through **WhatsApp** or a **contact form**—ideal for private orders, collaborations, press, and general inquiries.

The experience is intentionally **minimal and editorial**: generous space, warm sand-and-espresso tones, refined typography, and gentle motion so the page feels like a boutique window rather than a template.

---

## What you will find on the page

- **Home** — A hero that sets the tone: luxury, warmth, and a clear invitation to order or learn more.
- **Collection** — Signature fragrances with descriptions and a direct path to order via WhatsApp.
- **About** — The brand’s vision: craft, intention, and the idea of scent as a lasting impression.
- **Contact** — A simple form for collaborations, feedback, and inquiries, alongside email and WhatsApp.

Together, these sections answer three questions at a glance: *who we are*, *what we offer*, and *how to reach us*.

---

## BONHEURETERNAL in a sentence

A luxury perfume brand built on the belief that a great fragrance should linger—on the skin, in memory, and in how you are remembered.
